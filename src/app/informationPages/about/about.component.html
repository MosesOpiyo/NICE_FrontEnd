About Us

One stop shop

Nice Direct brings together a wide range of highly experienced partners who bring on board skills, resources and facilities needed to support direct coffee trade. They include cuppers, logisticians, warehousemen, shippers, and banks. Put together, they create a highly resourceful platform that provides a one stop shop for all direct coffee trade solutions.

Traceability

All Nice Direct coffees are single origin and traceable to farm for individual estates, or traceable to washing station for cooperative societies. You actually get to follow the coffee from flowering through to processing on the platform. All our producers post on a regular basis photos, videos and farm data on their timelines. This keeps you updated on the progress of the coffee in every stage and helps you make better purchasing decisions.

Relationships

At NICE we believe coffee is all about relationships. Connecting directly to farmers will help you build valuable relationships that will ensure a consistent and sustainable supply of coffee. The epitome of great relationships is travelling to origin which we regularly organize for our partners. Origin trips are great opportunities to strengthen valuable relationships that will enable you fulfill your dream of serving exceptional coffee.

How NiceDirect works

Quality control

Our presence at source ensures that all coffees the farmer brings to market directly are sustainably grown to give you the best possible cup.

Prefinances

We ensure the farmer gets prefinancing that allows them to get partial payment which enables them to continue working while the coffee goes to market.

Logistics

Our network of reliable logistics partners enables the farmer to ship their coffee to any part of the world and have it warehoused and delivered on order.

Marketing

We make noise on behalf of farmers to let you know their coffee is in the market. Joint marketing manages costs and allows the farmer to concentrate on what they do best.

Transparency

Everything from transaction costs, prices, and what finally ends up in the farmer's pocket is open for all transacting parties to view.

Shop

Once coffees are warehoused in the destination market, you can make orders for both green or roasted which can be fullfilled quickly and delivered to your doorstep.
